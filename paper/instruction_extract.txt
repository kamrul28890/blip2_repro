NLP Term Paper / Project
Assignment Scope & To-Do

Overview
This assignment is structured like a conference-style workflow and consists of:
• Paper selection and bibliography submission
• Reading and critique of papers
• Implementation and evaluation (reproduction)
• Final paper, peer reviews, and presentation

1. Paper Selection
Requirements
• Select at least 3 NLP papers
• Preferably from top venues: ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, etc.
• Ideally published within the last 3 years
• Must be approved by the professor

Submission Format
• Submit a single .bib file
• Filename: your userid.bib
• Each entry must include:
– Title
– Authors
– Venue
– Year
– Pages
– URL to PDF
Note: Do NOT upload the actual PDFs.
1

2. Reading and Understanding
• Read all three papers thoroughly
• Ensure at least one paper is suitable for implementation

3. Implementation and Evaluation
Requirements
• Implement at least one paper
• Must be non-trivial
• Guideline: approximately 4+ pages of code

Expectations
• Reproduce core ideas or methods
• Conduct meaningful evaluation
• Ideally replicate experiments (not required)

Notes
• Any programming language is allowed

4. Final Paper
Format
• Must follow ACL 2026 formatting (LaTeX)
• Submit a single PDF file
• Filename: your userid.pdf
• Do NOT include your name (double-blind)

Structure
• 50%: Paper Critique
– Analysis of all three papers
– Strengths, weaknesses, comparisons
• 50%: Project Section
– Implementation details
– Experimental setup
– Results and evaluation

Format
• In-class presentation
• Tools: PowerPoint, LaTeX Beamer, etc.

Content
• Summary of three papers
• Critique
• Implementation and evaluation

Notes
• Presentations begin early April
• Must be ready before final submission deadline
